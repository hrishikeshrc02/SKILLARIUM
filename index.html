OUR OFFERING

In India there is a dearth of skilled and experienced teachers. This problem is more pronounced in the rural areas.

The education system in India is mostly dominated by large coaching institutes who charge a lumpsum. Needless to say, the majority of parents cannot afford to pay for the tuition of their wards, and thus have to discard their dreams of admitting their children in esteemed higher educational institutes of the country.

We at Skillarium have created a platform to bring together students and educators. Via our solution, students will be able to avail of quality learning resources compiled by top educators of the country. Thhis facility will be provided at affordable costs in line with our hope to reach to all corners of rural India.

FEATURES AND PRICING

Why choose Skillarium?

FROM OUR CEO

Mr Hrishikesh Roy Choudhury

After I passed my +2, I had enrolled in a lot of online educational courses. It was then that I realised how in some subjects I could easily grasp the concepts watching online lectures while in other subjects I needed 1-on-1 guidance, but most of the time teachers weren’t available to provide me the assistance. Again, in college I realized how many tutors who have a great potential don’t get the proper exposure. So, me and my friends built this platform to bridge the gap between experienced and skilled educators and meritorious and tenacious students.
